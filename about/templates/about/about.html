About Me!

Hi, I'm Tilly and I created and designed the "Plant Life". I first looked at a plant-based diet when I experienced some minor health issues and realized my diet was possibly contributing to it. The more I delved into the plant world, the more I discovered the many health benefits of cooking with them. I'm excited to share with you what I have learned and look forward to any feedback about the ideas and recipes throughout the site.
